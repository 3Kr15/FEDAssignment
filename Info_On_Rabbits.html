10 most common Rabbit breeds:

Dutch

Small to medium-sized rabbits
with distinctive color patterns

Mini Rex

Rabbits with coat of short,
dense fur that are known for
their friendly nature

Holland Lop

Have distinctive lop ears with a
well-rounded body

Netherland Dwarf

One of the smallest rabbit
breeds having short, dense coat

Flemish Giant

One of the largest rabbit
breeds having gentle temperament

Lionhead

Small to medium sized rabbits,
known for their longer fur
around their heads

English Angora

Medium-sized and have
distinctive tufts of fur on thier
ears and long, soft coat of wool

Rex

Rabbits with short, plush coat
that stands upright

Jersey Wooly

Small rabbits known for their
friendly and docile nature

New Zealand White

Large, commercial breed known
for its all-white fur
